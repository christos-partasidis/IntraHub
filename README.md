# IntraHub - AI-Powered Digital Ecosystem for Inclusive Workplaces

## Project Overview
This repository presents IntraHub, an AI-powered digital platform designed to foster intergenerational collaboration, inclusion, and engagement in large organizations.  
Developed as part of the Digital Business Innovation (DBI) Lab at Politecnico di Milano in collaboration with Randstad Enterprise, the project addresses the growing need for digital adaptability and cross generational knowledge transfer in the financial sector.

## Project Mission
The goal of IntraHub is to build future ready workplaces by connecting employees through an intelligent ecosystem that integrates:
- Collaborative Web App - for community discussions, idea sharing, and peer learning  
- Integrated Digital Assistant (IDA) - AI-driven workflow support and expert matching  
- Gamification System - XP & badge based incentives to boost engagement and learning
  
---

## Key Features
- Cross-Generational Knowledge Sharing - connects senior expertise with junior innovation  
- AI Assistant Integration - contextual suggestions, feedback reminders, and smart notifications  
- Peer to Peer Expertise Hub — microlearning sessions and digital mentoring  
- Feedback Framework — continuous development culture with structured feedback templates  
- Gamified Engagement — recognition system that rewards collaboration and contribution  

---

## Impact
- Improved employee engagement and collaboration  
- Accelerated digital inclusion and workplace adaptability  
- Reduced turnover and knowledge loss  
- Demonstrated 2.5-year payback period through quantifiable ROI  

---

## Methodology
1. Context Analysis – PESTEL, Porter’s 5 Forces, RCBV, and Survey (150 respondents)  
2. Problem Mapping – generational inclusion, engagement, and digital skill gap  
3. Solution Design – web ecosystem + AI + gamification + privacy layer  
4. Implementation Plan – MVP rollout, training strategy, and economic feasibility
